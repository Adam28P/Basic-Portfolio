About Me

The name’s Adam Paralovos. I am a tireless seeker of knowledge, occassional purveyor of wisdom and also, coincidentally, a web and graphic designer. If you have a project that needs some creative injection then that’s where I come in!

My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add a personal touch to your product and make sure that it is eye-catching and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and identity in the most creative way.

When I'm not coding, designing or dabbling in pixels, you'll find me gaming, eating or watching The Simpsons.
